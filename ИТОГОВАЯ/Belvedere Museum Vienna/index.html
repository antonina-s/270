Experience the Belvedere
collection with Smartify

Unleash stories about epochal works from 800 years of Austrian art history with Smartify App. Enjoy free audio tours from the comfort of your home, explore and save your favorite objects, and share them online with your community:

Here's how it works:

  • Buy your museum ticket online and add the accompanying Smartify audio tour.
  • Receive the QR code and the tour link as a PDF.
  • Play the Smartify audio tour on your smartphone.
  • Immerse yourself in the history of the works.

Enjoy!
